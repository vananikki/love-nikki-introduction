Nikki

 Nikki's appearance often changes as a result of her being a stylist who participates in style battles. Her normal appearance is a fair-skinned teenage girl with waist-length pink hair, slightly wavy towards the end, that has navel-length forelocks and straightened bangs with a small split in the corner and warm, gentle amber eyes having a hint of pink and gold. Her casual attire consists of a white and pink floral lace dress, white socks, a pearl necklace with a red rose charm, and pink heart heels.
